Stone Gathering

Rules

Gathering stone during your downtime allows for you to hit a stone node a possible 20 times

Hit the stone node with absolute precision. A D6 is then rolled. Anything but a 1, returns stone equal to the dice rolled. There is a chance at receiving special resources during a Precision hit. Rolling a 1 on the D6 will give the player one stack on their Fail Count.

Hit the stone node as hard as possible! A D20 is then rolled. Any roll that is 6 or over returns stone equal to the dice rolled. There is NO chance at receiving special resources during a Hard Hit. Rolling a 5 or lower on the D20 results in the player receiving one stack to their Fail Count.
